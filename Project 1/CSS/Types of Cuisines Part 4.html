  2. Italian Cuisines

    3. Known for its simplicity and focus on high-quality, fresh ingredients. It features iconic staples like pizza, pasta, and risotto, often using olive oil, tomatoes, and basil.
  3. Punjabi Cuisines

    4. Originating from the fertile "land of five rivers," Punjabi cuisine is celebrated for its robust, earthy, and rich flavours. It is heavily influenced by the region's massive production of wheat, rice, and dairy.
  4. Gujarati Cuisines

    5. Gujarati cuisine, primarily vegetarian due to the influence of Jainism and Vaishnavism, is renowned for its signature balance of sweet, spicy, and tangy flavours. It is famously referred to as the "haute cuisine of vegetarianism" because of its creative use of everyday vegetables, grains, and pulses.
