Various Types of Foods

  1. Lentil Rice (Daal Bhat)

    Lentil Rice is a popular dish in India which is eaten as a diet in most parts of India.

  2. Vegetable Bread (Roti-Sabzi)

    Roti-Sabzi is usually eaten with Roti and Sabzi. Roti-Sabzi is made up of vegetables and bread.

  3. Cottage Cheese Curry (Paneer ki Sabzi)

    Cottage cheese curry is a punjabi dish in India.

  4. Vegetable Salad (Bhaji Koshimbir)

    Vegetable Salad is eaten with other meals or starters.
